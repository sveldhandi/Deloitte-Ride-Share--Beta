Safety & Trust
Built for colleagues, not strangers

Every safeguard here is designed with the understanding that this is a closed, company-verified network — not a public platform.

SOS

In any emergency, press the SOS button in the app to instantly alert your designated emergency contact and the Deloitte HR helpdesk with your last known location.

🔐

Deloitte-only access

Registration requires a verified @deloitte.com email and Employee ID. No outsider can access or view the platform.

📞

Masked contact sharing

Phone numbers are only revealed after both colleagues mutually accept the ride. In-app chat is available before acceptance so you can coordinate without sharing personal details.

🔔

Colleague status notifications

Stay informed at every step — "Your colleague has confirmed the ride", "Your colleague is on the way", "Ride completed". All notifications use colleague-first language.

👤

Mandatory photo ID

All users must upload a clear profile photo to enable visual recognition at the pickup point. Photos are verified against HR records.

🪖

Helmet policy for bikes

Bike rides require acknowledgment that both rider and pillion will wear helmets. Violations can be reported and will be escalated.

📋

Code of conduct

All users agree to Deloitte's Ride Sharing Code of Conduct on registration, covering behaviour, timeliness, and non-commercial intent.
